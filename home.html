Meet Will and Grace
    Will Grace Karen Jack
Will Truman

Will is a successful lawyer who is openly gay. He is intelligent, logical, and often serves as the voice of reason in his friendship with Grace. Will is confident and successful, but he can also be guarded and struggles with intimacy in his relationships. He has a complex relationship with his family, particularly his conservative mother.

Grace Adler

Grace is an interior designer who is Will's best friend. She is straight and often serves as the emotional counterpoint to Will's practicality. Grace is creative, artistic, and passionate, but she can also be insecure and impulsive. She has a complicated relationship with men, and throughout the series, she struggles to find a lasting relationship.

Karen Walker

Karen is Grace's assistant and friend, and she is a wealthy socialite who is often drunk or hungover. She is brutally honest, frequently insulting those around her, but she is also fiercely loyal to her friends. Karen has her own family issues, and she often relies on her friends to support her through personal crises. Despite her flaws, Karen is one of the most memorable characters in the show and often steals scenes with her sharp wit and biting sarcasm.

Jack McFarland

Jack is one of Will's closest friends and is a flamboyant, over-the-top gay man. He is loud, colorful, and loves musical theater. Jack is often the comic relief in the show, but he also has a serious side and experiences his own struggles with relationships and personal growth. He has a close relationship with Karen, and the two often get into hijinks together.
